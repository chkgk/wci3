Bets and decisions
{% if player.treatment == 1 or player.treatment == 3 or player.treatment == 5 %}

In each decision situation, you have to choose one of four different bets. The bets differ in how much they pay (hypothetically) based on the color of a randomly drawn ball from one of the boxes. Each bet specifies the box from which that ball is drawn and the payoffs depending on its color.

{% else %}

In each decision situation, you have to choose one of four different bets. The bets differ in how much you lose (hypothetically) based on the color of a randomly drawn ball from one of the boxes. Each bet specifies the box from which that ball is drawn and the losses depending on its color.

{% endif %}
